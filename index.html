About Us

We are a family-owned local business specializing in pressure washing, soft washing, and complete exterior cleaning. Loyal, friendly, punctual, and always prepared, Downstream Drip LLC is committed to restoring the beauty of homes and businesses with care and professionalism.

Our Services

  • Pressure Washing: High-powered water cleaning for driveways, sidewalks, patios, and other hard surfaces.
  • Soft Washing: Low-pressure cleaning ideal for siding, roofs, and fences. Safely removes algae, mold, and dirt.
  • Exterior Cleaning: Full-service exterior cleaning for windows, decks, gutters, and more — great for curb appeal.
